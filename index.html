Financial Formula — Net Present Value (NPV)

We use the standard NPV formula:

\[\mathrm{NPV} = \sum_{t=0}^{T} \frac{C_t}{(1 + r)^t}\]

Where: \(C_t\) are cash flows, \(r\) is the discount rate.
